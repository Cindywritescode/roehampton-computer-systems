A recommendation on which browser the EPL should recommend to their fans.

From the system performance analysis, we suggest recommending using Chrome. A Few factors come into play that made us come to this conclusion. Chrome used the least amount of CPU processing power when running all videos on average at different resolution compared to Edge and FireFox. This will give fans the benefit and ensure that users will have the best enjoyable experience when playing back a video and being played smoothly. This will also give the EPL to give a diverse viewing range at all resolutions provided.

The second most important factor is ensuring the viewer gets the best experience is to have the browser at a stable utilization and not stressing the system with a very high utilization even if its minor performance optimization being saved can help a lot when running and watching videos. We saw that chrome remain smooth when running at 2150p unlike Edge which stuttered greatly at 2160p as seen by the chart.
